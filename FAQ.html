FAQ

What do I learn from the lectures?


The lectures are designed as building blocks on advanced concepts, as a early exposure into more advanced concepts, giving you a decisive early advantage to excel!

We teach a variety of topics such as rotational motion, gravitation and magnetic field. We are mainly focused on physics, but we also teach mathematical skills like Ordinary Differential Equations (O.D.E) and Taylor Series

Do I have to attend every lecture?


Nope!

The lecture are stand-alone, easy to get into and meticulously crafted to be as engaging as possible.

The lectures are engaging no matter where you think your level of understanding is!

so don't be afraid to give it a shot and attend some lectures!

What is the purpose of the website?


The purpose of this website is meant to collate resources for students to easily access.From lecture notes to Physics Olympiad resources to Physics papers, this site is your go-to resource for everything advanced physics

As a staunch believer in open education, I strive to keep resources as open and accessible to all, as I believe everyone can excel, if they were given the chance and opportunity to do so.
