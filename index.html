Welcome to our Physics Hub at ASRJC!

We're a passionate student initiative dedicated to making the complexities of university-level physics accessible to everyone.

Dive into physics with our extensive collection of free resources, engaging lectures, and meticulously worked-through problems.

Whether you're gearing up for physics competitions like SPhO, SPhL, SYPT, or simply looking to strengthen your understanding of physics concepts, our materials are tailored to suit your needs. Join us on this exciting journey of discovery and register for our next lecture today!
